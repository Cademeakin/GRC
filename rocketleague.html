Rocket League

Rocket League, the One More Game simulator. Rocket League is a really fun game for people of all ages and skill-levels. The only downside is that you will play at least ten more games if you have decided to only play one before going to bed. The community is pretty toxic, but luckily it's not that hard to just not pay attention to the chat. The replay value is really high, especially because there are alot of different gamemodes and maps. You will always try to improve yourself, and you will notice yourself getting better when times moves on, especially when you learn how to fly. Easy to learn, hard to master! It is also an eSport! So there is always more to keep learning and potentially make a career out of playing this game. Highly recommend!
