Skyrim

This game was released on "November 11, 2011," and even after all this time, it is a game you can play over, and over, and over again. It is an extremely fun open world game that allows you to explore incredible places. You can go climb mountains, explore caves, discover settlements, meet new and strange people from traders to assassins. Best part? If you found somewhere but you're not in the mood to explore it, you can leave and come back later with fast travel allowing you to keep on exploring those beautiful regions. Another epic thing with this game, is you can never actually say you have "finished" it. You complete the story, but still have loads of quests left to do, items to sell, houses to build, lands to own etc. You will always keep thinking there is something more to do until you 100% finish the game. Community Mods and DLC bring so much more into this game, house building, new locations, deadly weapons, new people and quests etc. There are 244 quests in total across this game, not counting the DLC's and Community Mods. If you enjoy open world games, buy this game. Simple as that.
