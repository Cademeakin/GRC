About

Cade's Game Reviews is a website dedicated to making the best most accurate reviews for the best games on the market. Each game reviewed has had countless hours of blood, sweat and tears put into them to really draw the best experience out of them.

We review games from all over the spectrum, ranging from the Nintendo WiiU, to the main consoles like the PS4 and Xbox all the way to the master race; The PC. We only review the real deal though, so none of them mobile/tablet games will be on here. If you are not sure what game you are looking for and are just interested in the genre of the game, then using the "Game Genre" page will give you all the genre's you need.

If you have any inquiries questions, or propositions then please contact us using the Contact form at the bottom of each page. Enjoy the Reviews.
