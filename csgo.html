Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter Strike is most definitely my main game. With over 1500 hours in the game, I believe i am qualified to give a justified review of this game.
This game, first of all, may not be everybodies cup of tea. It is a very intense, competitive shooting game. It can be played around on for fun, but if you have a competitive nature, like me, you will just be wanting more, which is where the Competitive game mode comes into the picture. It is all about playing strategically with 4 other team mates with the goal of winning the game mode. There are many ranks, and it is unbelievably hard to make it to the top rank. This is what will keep you coming back for more. The grind to become the best player, and to get that top rank. And once that is reached, you can go higher and take this game into tournaments for huge prize pools, with the biggest being $1,000,000. This could be your future job!
