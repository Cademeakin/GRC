Borderlands 2

Borderlands 2 is an awesome game. It has an interesting story, great characters (especially the main villain Handsome Jack and your companion CL4P-TP aka Claptrap the mini robot) and some of the best gameplay I've ever experienced.
In a nutshell, you are a vault hunter and you are enlisted by a hyperactive CL4P-TP robot who calls himself Claptrap and refers to you as his minion. He wants you to help him bring down the villain Handsome Jack who is apparently responsible for shutting down the CL4P-TP production line.
You can play it single player, but at the same time open your game for up to three others so it becomes a co-op game, and it is at co-op that the game absolutely excels. The creatures on Pandora (where the game takes place) become stronger or weaker depending on how many players there are in a game.
As you progress through the story, you level up. Every mission you play, whether main or side mission has a certain preset level that would be nice for you to have making it clear in advance which missions are easier and harder. Expect more higher level missions as you progress. I think this system works very well indeed for this game. I had a ton of fun playing it, the dialogue is fantastic, the voice acting excellent, the diversity of characters and creatures is astoundingly large.
If you like FPS, humor, great story, great characters and excellently finetuned gameplay, this game is for you. If you liked Borderlands, you're gonna love Borderlands 2.
